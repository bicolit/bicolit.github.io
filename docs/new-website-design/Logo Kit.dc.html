Construction — 8×4 grid

Every tile snaps to a 10‑unit cell (viewBox 79.45×39.43). Tiles carry data-col / data-row so you can drive delays by position.

Clear space

Keep clear space of at least one grid unit (⅛ of the mark height) on every side.

Animation‑ready

The animated mark is 20 individual tiles, #p0–#p19 — 14 rounded squares and 6 arrow tips. Each carries data-col, data-row and data-idx, plus transform-box:fill-box so scales and rotations pivot around each tile’s own centre.

The four presets above (assemble, cascade, wave, pop) are just different ways of mapping those grid coordinates to an animation delay — a starting point for the motion work. The static placements (variants, sizes) reuse the mark as a single combined path.
